Covidosity

a quick overview of current COVID-19 conditions in potential travel destinations

Are you traveling to another state or country and need to know the current case trends of COVID-19 of your destination?

View Site View Repo

This is a site showing a quick overview of current COVID-19 conditions in potential travel destinations, condensed into 4 key numbers based on population. Built from scratch using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and jQuery as well as charts.js and moment.js.
As a bootcamp group-project, I was responsible for CSS design and styling using a Bulma Framework.
