Google Books Search

an app for searching for and saving books found in Google Books

Need a way to keep track of the books you'd like to read and have quick access to Google Books to purchase? Then, Google Books Search is for you.

View Site View Repo

A full MERN-stack application (Mongo, Express, React, Node) used for searching and saving books found in Google Books. I was responsible for complete site design and construction. A coding-bootcamp assignment, built using React, Bootstrap and the Google Books API with a Mongo database on the back-end.
