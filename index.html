Bio

full-stack developer based out of Houston

A creative and business-savvy web-developer with a passion for building clean, stream-lined, responsive websites and apps. With a technical background in the event industry, including more than 10 years as a project-manager, James has an abundance of experience working with a diverse base of corporate, nonprofit and government entities. After a fulfilling career in Event Technology, James decided to change course and persue a lifelong dream of learning Web Development where he could be creative and build things that last.

In 2020, he received a Certificate in Full-Stack Web Development from the University of Washington and he continues to develop his coding skills daily. His current project has him working as an intern Database Engineer on a MERN-stack app for Veterans and current Military Personnel.

James is very excited about the opportunities that await him as a Web Developer. He is looking for a position where he will be able to build amazing things and work with great people!
